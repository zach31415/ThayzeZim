====== Colors ======
So within Thayze, just as something to add another dimension to the storytelling capacity, colors represent abstract principles.  This allows for stronger theming and some more nuanced communications.

Yellow		The Absolute/other
Gold		Order/authority
Green		Nature/freedom
Blue		Truth/trust
Black		Devotion/loyalty
Red			Emotion/turbulence
White		Eternity/resilience
Grey		Knowledge/reasoning
Violet		Power/energy
