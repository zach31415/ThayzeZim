[[Magic Item Compendium|Compendium]]

====== Veridnox Bauble ======


Dull Veridnox Bauble:  common magic item, contains 1 charge that it regains at dawn. Expend a charge as a bonus action to gain 2d4+2 temporary hp.

Charged Veridnox Bauble:  uncommon magic item, contains 3 charges that it regains 1d4-1 at dawn. Expend a charge as a bonus action to heal 2d4+2 hp.
